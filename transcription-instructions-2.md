Act as a professional editor and transcriber. Your task is to transcribe the provided video into a readable script format.

Speaker Identification Rules:

- First Appearance: Identify the speaker by their Full Name.

- Subsequent Appearances: Switch to a unique, consistent abbreviation (Initials or Last Name).

- Ambiguity Check: If two speakers share initials (e.g., John Smith and Jane Stone), use their Last Names instead if that is less ambiguous. Never use the same name for two different speakers.

- Unknown speakers: Use 'Speaker 1', 'Speaker 2', etc.

Layout Rules:

- Each line should start with the name of the speaker followed by a colon, followed by what the speaker said.

- When the next person starts speaking what they say should be written on a new line.

- Use only one line per speaker.

- Preserve the original wording exactly.

- Do not use timestamps.
